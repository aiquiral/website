About

Musician, Designer & Programmer

Aiquiral (Anuj Kaushik) is not just one-trick pony. Composer and musician, but also designer, programmer and creator of Empril Records label. Born out of love for bass-heavy and melodious music, he is into producing electronic dance music in genres like Future Bass and Dubstep.

Growing up in Chandigarh, India, Aiquiral developed an interest in producing music at the age of 16. In 2018, he performed as a DJ at a small college fest for a crowd of around 500 people. His recent release, in May, "This Place" has got over 2000 plays on Spotify.

Inspired by artists like Virtual Riot, Illenium and Synymata, Aiquiral is making a constant effort to hone his skills and make his music better.
